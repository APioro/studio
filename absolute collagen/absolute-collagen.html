Absolute Collagen: Mobile Experience Redesign
PROJECT GOAL

As part of a university-led collaboration with Absolute Collagen, I was tasked with redesigning their mobile webshop to improve usability and visual coherence. The client needed simplified navigation, a clearer flow of product information, and a refreshed  UI. The unstructured product page inconsistent branding led to user drop-off. My scope involved tightening the visual identity, enhancing the product page UX,  and delivering a solution aligned with the brand’s tone and commercial goals.
DESIGN SOLUTIONS

To create a clean UI aligned with the brand’s modern tone I unified the color palette and limited gradients use. A clear typographic hierarchy was introduced to guide users more intuitively through content. On product pages, I replaced dense text with visual snapshots of key benefits, imagery, and infographics to boost clarity and engagement. Showcasing customer photos of the product from social media helped build trust and demonstrate real-life use. After conducting user reserch, I reorganized the content, making it easier to navigate the website.
